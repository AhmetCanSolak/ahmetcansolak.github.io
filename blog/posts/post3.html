A Small Sunday Game

5 February 2018

Yesterday was the last day before new spring semester. After breakfast, I suddenly realized have some spare time after a long while. My friends were still not arrived to campus, so I started to looking for a litte programming project to make my day more fun. Found a calm place with a nice view and sit. Then, Pygame came to my mind and jumped into implementing a small simple Tic-Tac-Toe game with that.

I had not used Pygame till yesterday but, thankfully, it is easy to learn and use. With the help of several Stack Overflow answers upon simple Google queries, I was able to finish a simple 3x3 demo implementation in a couple of hours.
